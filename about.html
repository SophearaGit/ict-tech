Our Story

ICT-Technology

ICTTech community was born from a simple realization: technology should feel exciting, not exhausting. Too often, finding the right phone, laptop, or accessory meant endless scrolling, confusing prices, and worrying about authenticity.

Founded 2021

Phnom Penh, Cambodia

How It Started

In 2021, ICT began as an educational initiative dedicated to teaching essential technology skills. Our programs covered a wide range of disciplines from programming languages such as Python and C++ to full‑stack development, cybersecurity, networking, and more. We built a community where students could gain practical knowledge and prepare for the digital future.

As our expertise grew, so did our vision. We recognized that technology is not only about learning how to build it, but also about making it accessible. With this in mind, ICT expanded beyond education to become a trusted destination for technology products.

Today, ICT offers a curated selection of devices — phones, earphones, power banks, and laptops — from leading global brands. Our mission remains the same: to empower people through technology, whether by teaching the skills to create it or by providing the tools to use it.
